CTF and Challenges

Password Attacks CTF

This series of challenges are made to practice some of the concepts covered in the post OSCPath Week 5: Antivirus Evasion and Password Attacks This game, like many other games, is organised in levels. Each level has access to the password of the next level. Your job is to somehow obtain that next password and level up.

Cryptography through the years CTF

Delve into the fascinating realm of cryptography with this captivating CTF challenge. This immersive adventure will take you on a thrilling quest to decipher cryptic messages, unravel ancient ciphers, and master the art of secret communication.
