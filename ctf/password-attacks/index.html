Password Attacks CTF - Level #0

Welcome to Level #0 of the Password Attacks CTF. This series of challenges are made to practice some of the concepts covered in the post OSCPath Week 5: Antivirus Evasion and Password Attacks.

This game, like many other games, is organised in levels. Each level has access to the password of the next level. Your job is to somehow obtain that next password and level up.

There are not special skills required to solve the levels. Sometimes you'll have to make your own scripts, use Kali Linux built in tools or even brute force your way. Don't give up if you find any difficulty, try harder!
